Tea Production Establishment

1989

NTEA first opened in 1989 as a small green tea shop. After many years and spending time improving the shop and collection of the best, healthiest, tastiest and rarest teas. NTEA started its production of teas and started wholesale sales.

Offering More Exclusive Teas

2004

NTEA offers the largest selection of teas in the world. The whole world started enjoying NTEA teas. They use them like medicines and for better health. And for tea lovers, there are also some of the rarest teas in the world.

Worldwide Recognition

2020

NTEA is recognized worldwide for the highest quality goods, the fastest delivery and a lot of love. our users not only enjoy but adore our products and our brand.
